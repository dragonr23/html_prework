New Hampshire

I grew up in the the lakes region of New Hampshire. We had everything you would want in a small New England town. Gunstock mountain was a 10 minute drive. There were three lakes surronding the area: Lake Opeechee, Lake Winisquam, and Lake Winipesaekee. Here I developed my love adventure and being outdoors.

I still spend time hiking, climbing and playing tennis up in New Hampshire when I find a free weekend.

Out into the world

I have been lucky enough to travel a lot for my age. I've traveled to many European Countries, Peru, and even a road trip across the USA! I have lived in Boston, New York, and San Francisco.
