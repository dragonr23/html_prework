About

Hi! My name is Rebecca Dragon. I just graduated from Northeastern University this past May. During my time at Northeastern, I was able to travel, work, and study in many amazing places!

In 2014, I started my journey in Thessaloniki, Greece. While in Greece, I fell in love with economics. I decided economics would be my major because it sought to understand the larger global forces that dictate our daily lives. I did a specialized economics program in Edinburgh, Scotland focused in macroeconomics.

As an economics major I felt inclined to work at many financial coops. I cooped at State Street Global Advisors, Gelfand, Rennert & Feldman, and finally Wellington Management Company. Although my experience opened the doors for many opportunities, I felt I was missing my need to be creative.

This is when I decided to start developing apps and learning swift! In between work and school, I self taught myself swift. I eventually used this skill at an intensive entrepreneurship program in San Fransisco. I found my true passion for development. The experience was life changing and I hope to one day start my own company.
